About Stress Check UP

Aside from the project being a final requirement for our
CS 180 - Artificial Intelligence
class, we recognize the fact that stress is quite common among college students, and we see the negative effects that varying degrees of stress has.

Doing this study, we found out that
stress, burnout, and fatigue
are different terms, commonly mistaken for one or the other. With this, we want to help users identify what they have and give them some tips on how to lessen them.

System


Classifier: Extremely Randomized Trees (Extra Trees)

This is a type of Randomized forest that operates by constructing a multitude of decision trees at training time and outputting the class that is the mode of the classes (classification) or mean prediction (regression) of the individual trees. Random decision forests correct the decision trees' habit of overfitting their training set. An "extra randomization" within the construction makes it an Extremely Randomized forest.

ExtraTreesClassifier implements a meta estimator that fits a number of randomized decision trees on various sub-samples of the dataset and use averaging to improve the predictive accuracy and control over-fitting. ExtraTreesClassifier are meant to tackle the variance problems (lack of robustness with respect to small changes in the training set) of individual DecisionTreeClassifier instances.
